Formula Logic

Transparent formulas for investor confidence.

Monthly Revenue = Subscription Fee × No. of Subscriptions
Total Variable Expense = Revenue × (Development & Infrastructure % + Marketing & Support % + Loss Reserve %)
Profit = Revenue − Variable Expense − Fixed Monthly Cost
Break-even Subscribers = Fixed Monthly Cost ÷ Contribution per Subscriber
Contribution per Subscriber = Fee × (1 − Total Variable Cost %)
Valuation = Weighted ARR Multiple + Weighted Profit Multiple
